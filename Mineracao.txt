boobs: a woman’s breast. Because I'm wearing a dress that accents my boobs.
pawn: a chess piece of the smallest size and least value. Each player has eight pawns at the start of a game. He had to sacrifice another pawn.
plea: a serious emotional request, especially for something needing action now. She made an emotional plea for her daughter's killer to be caught.
lesser: not as great in size, amount or importance as something/somebody else. A lesser woman would have given up.
madly: in a way that shows a lack of control. A madly ambitious businessman.
stake: money that somebody invests in a company. The government has a 10 percent stake.
humble: showing you do not think that you are as important as other people. Be humble enough to learn from your mistakes.
profess: to claim that something is true or correct, especially when it is not. They profess loyalty to the king.
afterthought: something done or said after other things because it was not thought of earlier (reflexão tardia). Accessibility and inclusivity cannot be an afterthought for media companies. 
wicked: morally bad. That was a wicked thing to do!
