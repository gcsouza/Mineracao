heiress: a female heir, especially one who has received or will receive a large amount of money. The girls in the photo are his daughters and heiresses to the Wilson fortune.
allegedly: expressed as though something is a fact but without giving any proof. She allegedly stole the money.
scammed: a dishonest way to make money by deceiving people. The company scammed hundreds of people out of their life savings.
conned: a dishonest trick that is done to get someone's money. We don't like being conned.
made up: created from the imagination : not true or real. That's all just made up to scare you.
larceny: the act of stealing something. She was born with larceny in her heart.
theft: the crime of stealing something from a person or place. A number of thefts have been reported recently.
hotline: a special phone line that people can use in order to get information or to talk about something. The company has an information hotline that customers can call if they are worried about products.
riveting: so interesting or exciting that it holds your attention completely. The court thanks you for the riveting demonstration.
fortress: a building or place that has been made stronger and protected against attack. They took refuge in the fortress.
