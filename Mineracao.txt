willingly: in a way that shows you are happy or pleased to do something or that you do not object to doing something. The student acted willingly.
underwhelming: not impressing or exciting you at all (decepcionante, desanimador). The evidence is underwhelming.
stunning: extremely attractive or impressive. You look absolutely stunning!
haute couture: the business of making fashionable and expensive clothes; the clothes made in this business (alta costura). In Portuguese it is known as haute couture.
grave: a place in the ground where a dead person is buried. She puts fresh flowers on her husband's grave every Sunday.
lobster: an ocean animal that has a long body, a hard shell, and a pair of large claws and that is caught for food. I had lobster for dinner.
yacht: a large sailing boat, often also with an engine and a place to sleep on board, used for pleasure trips and racing. A sailing yacht.
maxed out: estorou, no máximo, no limite, esgotei. I'm guessing you maxed out the plastic again.
adjoining: next to or joined to something. There is one pay phone adjoining the gas station.
sake: used to emphasize that it is important to do something or when you are annoyed about something (bem, causa, razão, interesse, benefício). Please marry her for our sake.
