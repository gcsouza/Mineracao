furthermore /ˌfɜːrðərˈmɔːr/: in addition to what has just been stated. Furthermore is used especially to add a point to an argument. And furthermore, I have come to a decision.
knowingly /ˈnəʊɪŋli/:  in a way that shows that you have special knowledge : in a knowing way (conscientemente, intencionalmente). I cannot knowingly contribute to your moral decay.
misrepresented /ˌmɪsˌreprɪˈzent/: to give information about somebody/something that is not true or complete so that other people have the wrong impression about them/it. This solution can easily be misrepresented to suit the will of the government.
edible /ˈedəbl/: suitable or safe to eat (comestível). The food at the hotel was barely edible.
arrangement /əˈreɪndʒmənt/: a plan or preparation that you make so that something can happen (acordo, arranjo, disposição, organização, esquema). But that arrangement may end tomorrow. 
hotshot /ˈhɑːtʃɑːt/: a person who is extremely successful in their career or at a particular sport (espertalhão, figurão, espertinho, sabichão). Listen, hotshot, I'm out as of now.
pitcher /ˈpɪtʃər/: the player who throws the ball to the batter (arremessador, lançador, atirador). He was the winning pitcher today.
scholarship /ˈskɑːlərʃɪp/: an amount of money given to somebody by an organization to help pay for their education. She won a scholarship to study at Stanford.
legroom /ˈleɡruːm/: the amount of space available for your legs when you are sitting in a car, plane, theatre, etc. There’s not much legroom in the front of the car.
hounding /haʊnd/: to keep following somebody and not leave them alone, especially in order to get something from them or ask them questions. I'm tired of them hounding me.
